How to measure

  1. Load a TEM image or use the MarsHill example.
  2. Click Detect scale bar and confirm the red dashed box marks the scale bar.
  3. Enter the scale-bar size in nm.
  4. Choose Straight or Wavy, then draw on the image.
  5. Review lengths in the Measurements panel.
  6. Export the CSV or save the annotated image.
